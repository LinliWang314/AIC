About

Aesthetic Intelligence Circle explores AI, creativity, and self-expression.

See the gatherings

Why it exists

AI can generate endlessly, but it still does not know what is beautiful, what matters, or what should be kept. AIC exists to bring AI into contact with taste, judgment, and self-expression.

Founder note

AIC began from a personal need for a different kind of space. As AI becomes more present in everyday creative and professional life, it can also make everything feel faster, louder, and harder to choose from. I wanted to create a gathering where people could slow down, think more carefully, and connect more deeply, not around hype or competition, but around taste, reflection, and the possibility of making something meaningful together.
